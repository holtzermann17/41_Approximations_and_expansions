Taylor series, derivation of


Let f⁢(x) be given by the following power series:

f⁢(x)=c0+c1⁢(x-a)+c2⁢(x-a)2+⋯+cn⁢(x-a)n+⋯=∑k=0∞ck⁢(x-a)k

Now let’s compute a few derivatives at x=a.

f⁢(a)=c0;f′⁢(a)=c1;f′′⁢(a)=2⁢c2;f(3)⁢(a)=6⁢c3;f(n)⁢(a)=n!⁢cn

From this, it is clear that cn=f(n)⁢(a)n!, thus the series can be written as:

Tn=∑k=0nck⁢(x-a)k=∑k=0nf(k)⁢(a)k!⁢(x-a)k

where f⁢(x)=T∞.
